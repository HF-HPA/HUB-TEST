BI Check List (Supervisors)

Key BI areas to review: inventory, negatives, comps, and sales performance.

Inventory & Negatives
  • Use the Consignment Report tab for roll-over inventory and units on hand.
  • Set ship date range from 1/1/2021 to current day for accurate inventory.
  • Sort inventory column from smallest to largest; review negatives and unusually large numbers.
  • Review sell-through by store, by SKU, and by category.
Sales & Comps
  • Use the Dashboard tab to review comp $$ and comp sell-through vs. last year.
  • Use the Monthly Sales tab to compare month-to-month performance.
  • Use Consignment Report (units) and Consignment Analysis (sales $$) as needed.
Transfers & Adjustments
  • On the Consignment Report tab, scroll to see transfer from / transfer to details.
  • Use adjustment / transfer fields to understand inventory movement between locations.
BI Features & Filters
  • Use drill down / drill up on each page (by store, SKU, category, supervisor).
  • Experiment with filters: product line, SKU, ship dates, ship-to, sales person, etc.
  • Click on any line to update charts/graphs to that selection; watch pie charts and graphs change.
